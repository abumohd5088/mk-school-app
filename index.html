About Our School

Excellence in Education

Milli Karwaan Public School, established in 2005, is committed to providing quality education that combines academic excellence with moral values. Our mission is to nurture young minds and prepare them to become responsible citizens and future leaders.

Located in Balrampur near Police Chowki, our school provides a safe and conducive learning environment with modern facilities and experienced faculty members. We follow the CBSE curriculum with innovative teaching methods that promote critical thinking and creativity.

Quality Education

CBSE Curriculum with modern teaching methods and smart classrooms

Experienced Faculty

Safe Environment

Secure campus with proper supervision and child protection policies

Smart classrooms, science labs, computer lab, and library

Academic Programs

Science

Our science program emphasizes hands-on learning through experiments and practical applications. Students learn scientific concepts through observation, experimentation, and critical thinking in our well-equipped science labs.

  • Physics, Chemistry, Biology
  • Well-equipped Science Labs
  • Science Projects & Exhibitions
  • STEM Education
  • National Science Day Celebrations
Mathematics

Our mathematics curriculum focuses on developing problem-solving skills and logical reasoning. We use innovative teaching methods and our dedicated maths lab to make mathematics engaging and accessible to all students.

  • Arithmetic, Algebra, Geometry
  • Maths Lab with Teaching Aids
  • Maths Olympiad Preparation
  • Maths Games & Puzzles
  • Real-world Applications
Language

Our language program develops reading, writing, speaking, and listening skills. We emphasize creative expression, comprehension, and effective communication in English, Hindi, and Sanskrit through various activities and competitions.

  • English, Hindi, Sanskrit
  • Reading Program & Library
  • Debate, Elocution & Declamation
  • Story Telling & Creative Writing
  • Spelling & Vocabulary
