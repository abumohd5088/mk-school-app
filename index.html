About Our School

Shaping Future Generations Since 2005

Milli Karwaan Public School in Balrampur has been a beacon of quality education, combining modern academic excellence with strong moral and Islamic values. Our mission is to develop well-rounded individuals who excel academically while maintaining strong ethical foundations.

We offer a comprehensive curriculum from Nursery to Class XII, following the CBSE pattern while incorporating Islamic studies and moral education. Our experienced faculty is dedicated to providing personalized attention to each student, ensuring their holistic development.

With state-of-the-art facilities, a safe learning environment, and extracurricular activities that foster creativity and leadership, we prepare our students to become responsible citizens and future leaders.
