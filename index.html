About Our School

Empowering Young Minds for a Brighter Future

Milli Karwaan Public School Balrampur, located at Boluha Police Chowki Balrampur, has been a beacon of quality education in the region since its inception. Our mission is to provide a nurturing environment where students can develop academically, socially, and emotionally.

We believe in holistic education that goes beyond textbooks. Our curriculum is designed to foster critical thinking, creativity, and character development. With a team of dedicated educators and state-of-the-art facilities, we strive to create lifelong learners who are prepared to face the challenges of the 21st century.

At Milli Karwaan, we celebrate diversity and promote values of respect, integrity, and excellence. Our students are encouraged to explore their passions, develop their talents, and become responsible citizens of tomorrow.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the school hours?

Our school operates from 8:00 AM to 3:00 PM, Monday through Friday. The morning assembly begins at 8:00 AM, and classes start at 8:15 AM. We have a 60-minute lunch break from 12:30 PM to 1:30 PM.

What is the admission process?

The admission process begins with submitting an application form available on our website or at the school office. Required documents include birth certificate, previous school records (if applicable), address proof, and passport-sized photographs. After document verification, there may be a brief interaction with the child and parents, followed by issuance of the admission confirmation.

What extracurricular activities are offered?

We offer a wide range of extracurricular activities including sports (cricket, football, basketball, athletics), arts (music, dance, painting, drama), academic clubs (science, mathematics, debate), and hobby classes (chess, robotics, gardening). Students can participate in multiple activities based on their interests and schedules.

Is transportation provided?

Yes, we provide transportation services with well-maintained buses covering various routes in Balrampur and nearby areas. The buses are equipped with GPS tracking, and each is accompanied by a trained attendant. Parents can enroll their children in the transportation service by paying the applicable fees.

What safety measures are in place?

Student safety is our top priority. We have 24/7 security personnel, CCTV surveillance throughout the campus, fire safety equipment, and regular safety drills. All staff undergo background checks, and we have strict visitor protocols. Our buses have GPS tracking, and we maintain a low student-to-teacher ratio for better supervision.

How are parents kept informed about their child's progress?

We maintain regular communication with parents through multiple channels: monthly progress reports, parent-teacher meetings (scheduled twice a year with optional additional meetings), a school mobile app for real-time updates, email communications, and our website portal. We also send notifications about upcoming events, homework, and important announcements.
