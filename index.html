About Our School

Learn more about our history, mission, and values

Our History

Founded in 2005, Milli Karwaan Public School has been a beacon of educational excellence in Balrampur for nearly two decades. Our journey began with a vision to provide world-class education to students in the region.

Our Mission

We are dedicated to fostering an environment where students can discover their potential, develop critical thinking skills, and become responsible global citizens who contribute positively to society.

Core Values

Integrity, Excellence, Innovation, and Community are the pillars that guide our educational philosophy. We believe in nurturing the whole child through a balanced approach to learning.

Academic Programs

Comprehensive curriculum designed for holistic development

Primary Education (Classes 1-5)

Our primary program focuses on building strong foundational skills in literacy, numeracy, and scientific thinking through interactive and engaging learning experiences.

  • Language Arts & Literature
  • Mathematics & Problem Solving
  • Environmental Studies
  • Art & Creative Expression

Secondary Education (Classes 6-10)

Students explore a broader range of subjects while developing critical thinking and analytical skills essential for higher education.

  • Science (Physics, Chemistry, Biology)
  • Mathematics
  • Social Studies
  • Computer Science
  • Foreign Languages

Senior Secondary (Classes 11-12)

Our senior secondary program offers specialized streams to prepare students for competitive examinations and university admissions.

  • Science Stream (PCM/PCB)
  • Commerce Stream
  • Humanities Stream
  • Career Counseling
  • Competitive Exam Prep

Admissions

Join our learning community and embark on a journey of excellence

Admission Process

Our admission process is designed to be simple and transparent:

  1. Fill out the application form
  2. Submit required documents
  3. Interaction with parents and child
  4. Admission confirmation

Applications are accepted throughout the year for most classes.

Required Documents

  • Birth Certificate
  • Previous School Records
  • Transfer Certificate (if applicable)
  • Four Passport-sized Photographs
  • Address Proof
  • Medical Certificate

Fee Structure

Our fee structure is competitive and transparent:

  • Primary Classes: ₹12,000/year
  • Secondary Classes: ₹18,000/year
  • Senior Secondary: ₹25,000/year

Special discounts available for siblings and early payments.
