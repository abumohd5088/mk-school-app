About Our School

Milli Karwaan Public School, established in 2005, is one of the leading educational institutions in Balrampur, providing quality education with a strong emphasis on Islamic values, academic excellence, and character development.

Our school is affiliated with the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE) and follows a comprehensive curriculum that balances modern education with Islamic teachings. We believe in holistic development, nurturing not only the intellect but also the moral and spiritual aspects of our students.

With a dedicated team of qualified teachers, state-of-the-art facilities, and a focus on individual attention, we create an environment where every child can thrive and reach their full potential.

Our mission is to produce knowledgeable, responsible, and compassionate citizens who contribute positively to society while maintaining their Islamic identity.

Admission Process

1

Application Submission

Visit our school office or download the admission form from our website. Fill in all required details and submit with necessary documents including birth certificate, previous school records, and recent photographs.

2

Document Verification

Our admission committee will verify all submitted documents. Please ensure all information is accurate and complete to avoid delays in the process.

3

Interaction Session

Shortlisted candidates will be invited for a brief interaction session with the principal and concerned teachers to assess readiness and compatibility with our school environment.

4

Admission Confirmation

Upon successful completion of all steps, parents will receive an admission confirmation letter along with details about fee payment and orientation program.
