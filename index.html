About Us

Learn more about our school's mission, vision, and values

Our Story

Milli Karwaan Public School was established in 2005 with the vision of providing quality education to children in Balrampur and surrounding areas. What began as a small institution with just 50 students has grown into a premier educational establishment with over 1,200 students and a dedicated staff of 60+ educators.

Our school is affiliated with the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE) and follows the NCERT curriculum, ensuring that our students receive a standardized, high-quality education that prepares them for national and international examinations.

We believe in holistic education that nurtures not just academic excellence but also character development, creativity, physical fitness, and social responsibility. Our approach combines traditional values with modern teaching methodologies to create well-rounded individuals ready to face the challenges of the 21st century.

Our Mission

To provide quality education that empowers students with knowledge, skills, and values necessary for personal excellence and responsible citizenship.

Our Vision

To be a center of academic excellence that nurtures creative thinkers, ethical leaders, and compassionate human beings.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our school

What are the school hours?

Our school operates from 8:00 AM to 2:30 PM on weekdays (Monday to Friday). Pre-primary classes (Nursery, LKG, UKG) conclude at 1:00 PM. Saturdays are half-days with classes from 8:00 AM to 12:30 PM.

What is the admission process?

The admission process begins with submitting an application form available at the school office or online through our website. Required documents include birth certificate, previous school records (if applicable), address proof, and four passport-sized photographs. After document verification, an interaction session with parents and child is conducted, followed by admission confirmation and fee payment.

Does the school provide transportation?

Yes, we provide transportation services with a fleet of well-maintained buses covering various routes in Balrampur and nearby areas. Our buses are equipped with GPS tracking and attendants to ensure student safety. Transport fees are additional to the tuition fees and vary based on distance.

What extracurricular activities are offered?

We offer a wide range of extracurricular activities including sports (cricket, football, basketball, athletics), performing arts (dance, music, drama), visual arts (painting, sculpture), literary activities (debate, elocution, creative writing), and clubs (science, robotics, environmental). Students can participate in multiple activities based on their interests.

How does the school handle student safety?

Student safety is our top priority. We have CCTV surveillance throughout the campus, trained security personnel at all entrances, a strict visitor management system, and designated staff for student supervision during breaks and activities. Our buses have tracking systems and attendants. We conduct regular safety drills and have a comprehensive child protection policy in place.
