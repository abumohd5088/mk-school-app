About Milli Karwaan Public School

Established in 1995, Milli Karwaan Public School Balrampur has been committed to providing quality education with a focus on character building and holistic development. Our school follows the CBSE curriculum and emphasizes both academic excellence and co-curricular activities.

We believe in nurturing each child's unique talents and potential, preparing them to face the challenges of the 21st century with confidence and integrity. Our teaching methodology combines traditional values with modern educational techniques to create a balanced learning environment.
