About Milli Karwaan Public School

Established in 2005, Milli Karwaan Public School has been committed to providing quality education to children in Balrampur and surrounding areas. Our mission is to nurture young minds and prepare them for a successful future through innovative teaching methods and holistic development programs.

We believe in creating a learning environment that fosters academic excellence, character development, and social responsibility. Our experienced faculty members are dedicated to helping each student reach their full potential.

Academic Programs

Mathematics

Our mathematics curriculum focuses on developing problem-solving skills, logical reasoning, and numerical proficiency through interactive learning methods.

Science

Hands-on experiments, science fairs, and project-based learning help students understand scientific concepts and develop critical thinking skills.

Languages

We offer comprehensive language programs in English, Hindi, and Sanskrit, focusing on reading, writing, speaking, and listening skills.

Arts & Music

Creative expression is encouraged through visual arts, music, dance, and drama programs that nurture students' artistic talents.

Computer Science

Our computer labs are equipped with modern technology, and our curriculum includes programming, digital literacy, and internet safety.

Physical Education

Regular sports activities, yoga, and fitness programs promote physical health, teamwork, and sportsmanship among students.

School Facilities

Library

Our well-stocked library has thousands of books, periodicals, and digital resources to support students' reading and research needs.

Science Labs

Equipped with modern instruments and safety features, our science labs provide hands-on learning experiences in physics, chemistry, and biology.

Computer Labs

State-of-the-art computer labs with high-speed internet and the latest software support digital learning and programming education.

Sports Facilities

Our school has a large playground, basketball court, and indoor games room to promote physical fitness and sportsmanship.

Music & Dance Room

Dedicated spaces for music and dance education with instruments, sound systems, and professional-grade equipment.

Medical Room

Our medical room is staffed with trained personnel and equipped to handle minor injuries and health emergencies.

Admissions

We welcome new students throughout the academic year. Our admission process is designed to be simple and transparent, ensuring a smooth transition for both students and parents.

Eligibility

Children between the ages of 3-16 are eligible for admission based on their age and previous academic records.

Documents Required

Birth certificate, previous school records, address proof, and passport-sized photographs.

Admission Process

Submit application → Document verification → Interaction session → Admission confirmation.
