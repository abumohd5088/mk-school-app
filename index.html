About Milli Karwaan Public School

Milli Karwaan Public School Balrampur is a premier educational institution dedicated to providing quality education and holistic development for students. Established with a vision to create future leaders, our school combines traditional values with modern teaching methodologies.

Located at Boluha Police Chowki Balrampur, our campus provides a safe, nurturing, and stimulating environment for learning. We believe in the all-round development of students, focusing not only on academic excellence but also on character building, creativity, and physical well-being.

1200+ Students
50+ Teachers
15+ Years of Excellence

Our Mission

To provide quality education that nurtures intellectual curiosity, critical thinking, and moral values, preparing students to become responsible citizens and leaders in a global society.

Our Vision

To be a center of academic excellence that fosters holistic development, innovation, and lifelong learning, creating well-rounded individuals who contribute positively to society.
