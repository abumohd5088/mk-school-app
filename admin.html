About Our School

Excellence in Education

Milli Karwaan Public School, established in 2005, is committed to providing quality education that combines academic excellence with moral values. Our mission is to nurture young minds and prepare them to become responsible citizens and future leaders.

Located in Balrampur near Police Chowki, our school provides a safe and conducive learning environment with modern facilities and experienced faculty members.

Academic Programs

Science

Our science program emphasizes hands-on learning through experiments and practical applications. Students learn scientific concepts through observation, experimentation, and critical thinking.

  • Physics, Chemistry, Biology
  • Science Labs
  • Science Projects
  • Science Exhibition
Mathematics

Our mathematics curriculum focuses on developing problem-solving skills and logical reasoning. We use innovative teaching methods to make mathematics engaging and accessible to all students.

  • Arithmetic, Algebra, Geometry
  • Maths Lab
  • Maths Olympiad
  • Maths Games
Language

Our language program develops reading, writing, speaking, and listening skills. We emphasize creative expression, comprehension, and effective communication in English and other languages.

  • English, Hindi, Sanskrit
  • Reading Program
  • Debate & Elocution
  • Story Telling
